About Me

Hi, I’m Suna, an almost 37-year-old beginner in coding with a journey full of twists and turns.

I was born and raised in China, where I lived until I turned 20. In 2008, I moved to Denmark to further my education in Multimedia Design and E-Concept Communication. Ironically, despite earning my degree, I walked away with very little knowledge in the field—God knows how I managed! During my studies, I fell in love with the food and beverage industry, a passion that led me to work in that field alongside my education from 2008 to 2014. After completing my studies, I returned to China and continued in the food and beverage industry until 2017. But my story doesn’t end there!

At the end of 2017, I moved to France with zero knowledge of French and started my life from scratch. After plenty of hard work (and learning the language—you know how it goes), I built a new life. In 2023, a health issue forced me to step away from the food and beverage industry. During my recovery, I started exploring new career paths and stumbled upon coding. I don’t remember exactly why I tried it, but once I did, I fell in love with it. That’s when I decided to "pick up" the threads of my initial education in multimedia design from Denmark—this time with a clear goal and renewed passion. Now, I’m committed to building a future in web development.

I’ve set a goal to acquire solid coding skills before beginning a Web Developer training program on February 17th, 2025. By then, I aim to design fully functional websites and create a portfolio that will open the door to exciting new opportunities.
